Patient Experience

Patient Portal Redesign for Regional Health System

Challenge

A regional health system serving over 500,000 patients struggled with low patient portal adoption (22%) and high support call volume. Patients found the portal confusing and couldn't complete basic tasks like scheduling appointments or accessing test results.

Our Approach

  • Conducted user research with 40+ patients across diverse demographics
  • Observed provider workflows and support call patterns
  • Mapped current vs. ideal patient journeys
  • Designed and tested new information architecture
  • Created accessible, mobile-first interface
  • Developed design system for consistency

Solution

We redesigned the entire portal experience with clear navigation, simplified workflows, and accessible design. Key features included one-tap appointment scheduling, conversational test result presentation, and plain-language health education.

Provider Experience Optimization for Hospital System

Challenge

Provider burnout was rising, with documentation burden cited as the primary factor. The hospital system needed to redesign clinical workflows and EHR interactions to give providers time back.

Our Approach

  • Shadowed providers across specialties
  • Timed and analyzed documentation workflows
  • Identified redundant data entry and unnecessary clicks
  • Co-designed solutions with clinical teams
  • Prototyped and tested improvements
  • Measured time savings

Solution

We streamlined clinical documentation, reduced redundant fields, and redesigned order entry flows. Smart defaults and intelligent templates reduced clicks by 60% while maintaining compliance.

Telehealth Design

Telehealth Platform for Multi-Specialty Practice

Challenge

A multi-specialty practice needed to rapidly deploy telehealth during the pandemic, but their initial solution had poor patient adoption and frustrated providers. They needed a complete redesign that worked for diverse patient populations and clinical specialties.

Our Approach

  • Analyzed failed telehealth sessions to identify barriers
  • Researched technology access across patient populations
  • Designed for low digital literacy and limited bandwidth

  • Built comprehensive pre-visit testing and support
  • Ensured HIPAA compliance and accessibility

Solution

We designed a telehealth platform that removes technical barriers. Clear pre-visit instructions, automated connection testing, one-click join, and real-time technical support ensure successful visits across all patient populations.

Website Redesign for Community Health System

Challenge

A community health system's outdated website was failing to serve its diverse patient population. Navigation was confusing, content wasn't accessible, and mobile experience was poor. The website wasn't reflecting the quality of care provided.

Our Approach

  • Conducted user research with patients and community members
  • Audited existing content and information architecture
  • Designed mobile-first responsive experience
  • Rewrote content for health literacy and cultural sensitivity
  • Ensured full WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
  • Integrated appointment scheduling and wayfinding

Solution

We created a welcoming, accessible website that reflects the health system's commitment to community care. Clear pathways guide patients to the right services, multilingual content serves diverse populations, and integrated tools make scheduling and navigation simple.
