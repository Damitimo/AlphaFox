Journey Mapping Services:

Comprehensive Journey Maps

  • Multi-channel touchpoint analysis
  • Emotional journey visualization
  • Pain point identification
  • Service blueprint development

Specialized Journey Maps

  • Condition-specific journeys (oncology, chronic disease, maternal health)
  • Care continuum mapping (prevention through aftercare)
  • Cross-system journey analysis
  • Family and caregiver journeys
  • Provider experience journeys

Journey Optimization

  • Transition point improvement
  • Communication optimization
  • Digital touchpoint enhancement
  • Service delivery recommendations

Deliverables:

  • Visual journey maps
  • Persona-specific experiences
  • Prioritized opportunities
  • Quick-win recommendations
  • Long-term strategic roadmap
