Process

What to expect

We've designed a hiring process that respects your time and gives you a real sense of who we are. Here's what you can expect.

1 — Application Review

We review every application carefully. We're looking for healthcare passion, design excellence, and alignment with our values.

2 — Initial Conversation

A 30-minute video call to get to know each other, discuss your experience, and explore mutual fit.

3 — Design Challenge

A paid, practical design exercise relevant to real healthcare challenges. We respect your time.

4 — Team Interviews

Meet the team you'd be working with. We want you to get a real sense of our culture and approach.

5 — Offer & Onboarding

If it's a mutual fit, we'll make an offer and welcome you to the team with comprehensive onboarding.
